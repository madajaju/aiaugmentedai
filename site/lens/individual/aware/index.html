Diagnosis

Is this really you?

You are not behind. You are at the point where awareness needs to become a simple daily behavior.

  • You have tried ChatGPT or another AI tool.
  • You use AI occasionally, but not consistently.
  • You know AI matters, but you are unsure where to begin.
  • You still do most of your work manually.
  • You are curious, but you do not yet have a repeatable workflow.

This week

Your mission this week

You do not need to master AI yet. You need one successful habit.

  • Pick one repetitive daily task.
  • Use AI on that task every day this week.
  • Keep a short note about what worked and what failed.
  • Repeat before adding another AI use case.

Example Scenario

Common mistakes

  • Trying too many tools at once.
  • Expecting perfect answers.
  • Giving up after one bad response.
  • Mistaking curiosity for capability.
  • Measuring activity instead of improvement.

AI posture

Use AI intentionally, validate your outputs, and keep human judgment in the loop.
