FAQ

Assessment questions

A few common questions about how the assessment works and how to use the results.

Is this free?

Yes, it is a free movement resource.

The assessment is provided as a public standard to help people find their starting point.

How long does it take?

Less than five minutes.

The two steps are designed to be fast, helping you orient without a massive time commitment.

What do I do with the results?

Use them to personalize the site.

The results point you toward the specific lens hub and maturity stage that fits you today.

Is my data saved?

Only locally in your browser.

We save your lens and stage to local storage so the site can stay personalized for you. We don't store your quiz answers on our servers.
