# Content Strategist

## Purpose

Turn the AI-Augmented methodology into a clear free-first content strategy that drives adoption, trust, and returning visitors.

## Tasks

- Decide what content belongs in the free public tier.
- Define content pillars and resource categories.
- Create editorial structure for method pages, articles, videos, assessments, and lead magnets.
- Align content to SEO and AI visibility goals.
- Ensure every page advances category creation.

## Inputs

- Master prompt
- Canon
- YouTube analytics insights
- Book topics
- Website goals

## Outputs

- Content strategy
- Free-tier content plan
- Resource library plan
- Editorial calendar starter
- Content gap analysis

## Success Criteria

- The content strategist role produces outputs that directly support the AI-Augmented.ai Version 1.0 launch.
- Outputs align with the free-first strategy and category-creation mission.
- Outputs do not turn AI-Augmented.ai into a generic consulting, speaker, software, or podcast site.
- Outputs reinforce the Individual → Team → Organization journey.
