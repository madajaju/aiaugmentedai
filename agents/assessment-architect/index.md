# Assessment Architect

## Purpose

Design the free introductory assessment experience and prepare the architecture for future paid detailed reports through shop.paidar.ai.

## Tasks

- Define Individual, Team, and Organization assessment flows.
- Create starter question categories and scoring model placeholders.
- Design result levels and plain-language explanations.
- Separate free assessment value from paid detailed reports.
- Identify future integrations for email capture, CRM, and shop.paidar.ai.

## Inputs

- Canon
- Free-first strategy
- Monetization strategy
- User journey
- Assessment goals

## Outputs

- Assessment architecture
- Question category model
- Scoring framework
- Results page outline
- Paid upsell boundary

## Success Criteria

- The assessment architect role produces outputs that directly support the AI-Augmented.ai Version 1.0 launch.
- Outputs align with the free-first strategy and category-creation mission.
- Outputs do not turn AI-Augmented.ai into a generic consulting, speaker, software, or podcast site.
- Outputs reinforce the Individual → Team → Organization journey.
