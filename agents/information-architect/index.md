# Information Architect

## Purpose

Design the site structure, navigation, page hierarchy, and content relationships so visitors understand the Individual → Team → Organization journey.

## Tasks

- Create the sitemap and navigation model.
- Define page templates and section-level content flow.
- Map visitor journeys from curiosity to assessment to newsletter to books/training/community.
- Design resource taxonomy for articles, videos, podcasts, worksheets, templates, and case studies.
- Ensure pages support future expansion without restructuring.

## Inputs

- Master prompt
- User journey
- Canon mapping
- Content inventory
- Business ecosystem map

## Outputs

- Sitemap
- Navigation structure
- Page hierarchy
- Content taxonomy
- User journey map

## Success Criteria

- The information architect role produces outputs that directly support the AI-Augmented.ai Version 1.0 launch.
- Outputs align with the free-first strategy and category-creation mission.
- Outputs do not turn AI-Augmented.ai into a generic consulting, speaker, software, or podcast site.
- Outputs reinforce the Individual → Team → Organization journey.
