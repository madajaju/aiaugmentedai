# Brand Strategist

## Purpose

Define the AI-Augmented brand position and ensure the site clearly separates AI-Augmented.ai from Paidar.ai, DrDarrenSpeaks.com, shop.paidar.ai, and EmbracingDigital.org.

## Tasks

- Clarify brand hierarchy: AI-Augmented → AI-Augmented Method → assessments/books/training/certification/community.
- Define voice, tone, promise, and differentiation.
- Prevent the site from becoming a generic AI consultancy, speaker site, or training site.
- Create brand messaging pillars and homepage positioning language.
- Ensure Dr. Darren is positioned as founder, not the center of the site.

## Inputs

- Master prompt
- Existing brand assets
- Paidar.ai
- DrDarrenSpeaks.com
- EmbracingDigital.org
- Canon terminology

## Outputs

- Brand positioning brief
- Messaging hierarchy
- Voice and tone guide
- Homepage positioning copy
- Do/don’t brand rules

## Success Criteria

- The brand strategist role produces outputs that directly support the AI-Augmented.ai Version 1.0 launch.
- Outputs align with the free-first strategy and category-creation mission.
- Outputs do not turn AI-Augmented.ai into a generic consulting, speaker, software, or podcast site.
- Outputs reinforce the Individual → Team → Organization journey.
