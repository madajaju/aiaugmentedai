Method

AI-Augmented Method

AI-Augmentation is the practice of using AI to improve human work without removing human accountability.

AAOS is the framework for the AI-Augmented Method. Use the lens selector to view the method through the Individual, Team, or Organization context that matches your situation.

Core artifact

The Integrity Packet is the unit of reliable work.

The AAOS framework treats the Integrity Packet as the transfer-ready unit that carries evidence, assumptions, ownership, and context.

What it does

An Integrity Packet makes consequential work safe to review, challenge, transfer, approve, and audit without reconstructing the author's private context.

What belongs in it

Grounding, evidence, assumptions, ownership, validation status, and a clear handoff trail. For individual work, this becomes the Individual Integrity Packet.

Stages

Diagnose, Activate, Controls, Execute, Measure, Scale.

The AAOS lifecycle is sequential. Each stage is a prerequisite for the next. The core model stays the same across individual, team, and organization; the artifact depth and tools change by lens.

Stage detail

The same stages, different tools.

Individual work uses the IIP and personal workflow tools. Team work uses shared controls and validation depth. Organization work adds governance, approved tools, and enterprise measurement.

Diagnose

Use the individual readiness assessment and AI Experience x SME thinking to identify whether you are operating as an AI User, AI Operator, or Augmented Architect in the task at hand.

Activate

Turn the diagnosis into a bounded practice plan: strengthen the weakest axis, refine your personal AI stack, and make the next workflow smaller and clearer.

Controls

Use the Individual Integrity Packet, visible assumptions, and self-validation habits to keep output inspectable before you share it.

Execute

Run the personal workflow through draft, grounding, validation, handoff, and learn. Keep the check visible instead of relying on memory.

Measure

Track correction load, validation pass rate, and posture movement with a personal performance dashboard. Measure whether quality is improving, not just speed.

Scale

Replicate what works into more tasks only when the check is stable and the IIP can be reused without losing context.

Diagnose

Use the team maturity assessment and posture matrix to locate where the team is reactive, controlled, integrated, or optimized by workflow.

Activate

Translate the diagnosis into shared capability work: common prompts, templates, rubrics, and explicit task ownership across the group.

Controls

Apply the validation ladder, named validation owner, and packet completion rules so review depth matches consequence.

Execute

Run the integration loop with transfer-ready work, clear handoffs, and the Integrity Packet as the standard evidence chain.

Measure

Use the operating dashboard and handoff return rate to see whether the team is becoming reliable across cycles, not just productive in bursts.

Scale

Codify what is working into shared standards so the team does not depend on one expert carrying the quality function.

Diagnose

Use the enterprise AI posture map to classify readiness, fragility, over-adoption, and under-leveraged expertise across workflows and business units.

Activate

Connect the strategic AI thesis, decision rights, and talent architecture to the workflows where AI should create advantage and where it should stay constrained.

Controls

Define governance stack, consequence tiers, validation ownership, exception paths, and the enterprise Integrity Packet standard for consequential work.

Execute

Deploy approved tools, role architecture, training records, and integrity packet flow so execution is repeatable at enterprise scale.

Measure

Track trust velocity, drift, correction load, and the enterprise operating dashboard so leaders can separate real reliability from adoption theater.

Scale

Use scale readiness evidence, replication plans, and updated readiness profiles to expand only when control stability holds under pressure.

Validation ladder

Prepared By, Validated By, Approved By.

Human decision-making stays explicit. AAOS keeps the roles separate so the work can be checked and signed off without ambiguity.

Prepared By

The person who generates the augmented output and assembles the evidence into a usable packet.

Validated By

The person who checks the evidence, assumptions, and completeness before release moves forward.

Approved By

The person who accepts residual risk and authorizes the consequential handoff.
